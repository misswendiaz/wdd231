Frequently Asked Questions

What is Tutor Buddy?

Tutor Buddy is a lightweight web app designed to help tutors log, manage, and compute session data—including date, duration, rate, and payment balance.

Who is Tutor Buddy for?

It’s ideal for individual tutors or small tutoring services who want a simple and offline-capable way to track sessions without relying on spreadsheets.

Do I need to create an account?

No account or login is required—data is stored locally in your browser's localStorage. This makes it private and accessible even offline.

How do I log a session?

Go to the Sessions page, fill in the required fields (date, times, subject, etc.), and click “Save Session.”

Can I edit a session after logging it?

Yes! Click the “Edit” button next to any session in the table to update details or correct mistakes. After editing, click “Update Session.”

Can I delete a session?

Yes — click “Delete” on a session row. You can undo the deletion within a short time period using the notification prompt.

How do I mark multiple sessions as paid?

Use the session filters to select sessions, then click the “Mark Filtered as Paid” button to process them at once.

Can I request a new feature?

Absolutely! Choose “Feature Suggestion” or “Feature Enhancement” in the Contact form and describe what you'd like to see—your input helps us improve.
